NAPOLI: THE PRIDE OF SOUTHERN ITALY

SSC Napoli, founded in 1926 (with roots dating back to 1905), is the most successful football club in Southern Italy, based at the Stadio Diego Armando Maradona in Naples. Nicknamed the Azzurri or Partenopei, the club reached its golden era in the 1980s with Diego Maradona, and has achieved significant success.
